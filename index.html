About

Welcome to my personal blog on ice cream. I'm Katelin kasemersky and I'm from RVA. I would consider myself an ice cream connoiseur as I have tried many locations in RVA and so many flavors. Thus, I feel I have a pretty accurate understanding of the best ice cream locations in RVA and I wanted to share so that others with a sweet tooth as big as mine can find their new favorite ice cream shop and flavor!

Gelati Celesti

Gelati Celesti has been one of my favorite ice cream places for years! The ice cream is very rich and decadent. They have many unique seasonal flavors and delicious ice cream sandwiches made to-go.

Brusters

Brusters is a staple ice cream spot in RVA. There are many locations all over town and they have consistent quality and taste. They offer many simple flavors and many funky flavors. Brusters also offers delicious milkshakes and blasts which are a blend of ice cream and any mix ins you want swirled together.

Bev's Homemade

Bev's is a long-standing homemade ice crema shop in the heart of Carytown. The pink exterior and sign has been a staple for the shop for many years. If you are looking for simple and delicious ice cream flavors and some homemade whip cream, then you have found the spot!

Jeni's Splendid Ice Cream

Jennis Ice Cream is one of my new favorites recently. They offer very unique flavors that I have not had at any other ice cream locations. In my opinion, I think that Jeni's ice cream is a blend of Brusters and Gelati Celesti into one. jeni's ice cream is very rich and creamy while still have lots of yummy chunks of mix-ins and many speciality flavors.

My ice cream reccommendations!

  • Gelati Celesti: Salted Caramel, The Dude, Just Asked, Raspberry
  • Brusters: Graham Cracker, Cholocate Raspberry Truffle, Cotton Candy
  • Bev's: Coffee Oreo, Strawberry, Cookies and Cream
  • Jeni's:Green Mint Chip, Brown Butter Almond Brittle, Goey Butter Cake

New flavors everyone should try!

  • Gelati Celesti: Butter Pecan
  • Brusters: Cinnamon Bun
  • Bev's: Raspberry Chip
  • Jeni's: Brambleberry Crisp
